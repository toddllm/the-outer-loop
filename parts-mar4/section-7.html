Source →

What This Means

A robot AI agent that understands physical space (where things are) and temporality (how things change over time) - the two capabilities robots have most lacked. Running on the Unitree G1 (a ~$16,000 humanoid robot) and being fully open source means anyone can build on this. This is the open-source model applied to robotics: instead of one company controlling robot intelligence, it becomes shared infrastructure.

Source →

What This Means

A lost 1897 film by Georges Méliès (the pioneering filmmaker depicted in the movie "Hugo") features cinema's first robot. In a newsletter about AI and robotics, this is a cultural bookend - humans have been imagining artificial beings for at least 129 years. We're now building what Méliès could only film as fantasy.
