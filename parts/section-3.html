What This Means

Anthropic made it easy to bring your conversation history from ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot into Claude. This is the AI equivalent of phone number portability in telecom - it lowers the switching cost to nearly zero, which accelerates the user exodus from competitors.

1
In ChatGPT, click your profile (bottom-left) → Settings → Data Controls → Export Data. Click "Confirm export." You'll receive an email within 5-30 minutes with a download link to a ZIP file containing conversations.json and any uploaded files.
2
In Claude (claude.ai/settings), scroll to Import Data and upload the ZIP. Claude parses your history and shows "X conversations imported." This takes under a minute.
3
Your ChatGPT conversation history now appears in Claude's sidebar. Claude uses it to personalize future responses - it already knows what you've been working on.

Also works with Google Gemini and Microsoft Copilot exports. The entire process is about 5 minutes of active work.

Quantum entanglement is a phenomenon where two particles become linked so that measuring one instantly reveals the state of the other, no matter how much distance separates them. This connection means that observing a property of one particle immediately tells you what property its partner has, without any physical signal traveling through space between them. Despite this instant correlation across space, it cannot be used to send information faster than light because each individual measurement result remains random and unpredictable.

def is_prime(num):
    """Check if a number is prime."""
    if num < 2:
        return False
    for i in range(2, int(num ** 0.5) + 1):
        if num % i == 0:
            return False
    return True

The Supreme Court declined to hear an appeal seeking copyright protection for AI-generated artwork, cementing a legal regime in which purely AI-generated works cannot receive copyrights.
Source →

What This Means

By refusing to hear the case, the Supreme Court let stand the rule that AI-generated works - images, text, code, music - cannot be copyrighted if no human was meaningfully involved in creating them. This has huge implications for businesses: your AI-generated marketing copy, product images, or code may have no legal protection. A competitor could copy them freely. It creates a strong incentive to keep humans in the creative loop.
